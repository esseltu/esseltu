Find A Vendor

Mobile App

Description: The Mobile Money Vendor Locator is a user-friendly application designed to help individuals find mobile money vendors in their vicinity with ease. This project aims to bridge the gap between users and mobile money services by providing a seamless and efficient way to locate vendors based on their current location.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Location Tracking: Utilizes GPS technology to pinpoint the user’s current location and display nearby mobile money vendors.
  • Vendor Information: Provides detailed information about each vendor, including their name, address, contact details, and operating hours.
  • User Reviews and Ratings: Allows users to read and leave reviews and ratings for vendors, helping others make informed decisions.
  • Search and Filter Options: Enables users to search for vendors by name or filter results based on distance, ratings, and services offered.
  • Interactive Map: Displays vendors on an interactive map, making it easy for users to visualize their options and navigate to their chosen vendor.

Objective: To enhance the accessibility and convenience of mobile money services by creating a reliable and intuitive platform for locating mobile money vendors.
